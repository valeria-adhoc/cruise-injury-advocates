Slip, Trip & Fall Onboard

Back to services

Service highlights

Wet decks, pool surrounds, stairwells, and poorly maintained walkways cause some of the most common and most serious injuries aboard a cruise ship.

What we investigate

We move quickly to preserve evidence: incident reports, maintenance and inspection records, surveillance footage, crew statements, and the ticket contract that governs your claim.

How we build your case

From the first call, our attorneys handle the cruise line and its insurers directly, document your injuries and losses, and prepare every claim as if it will be tried.

Deadlines are short

Cruise tickets often shorten the time you have to file and require early written notice. Most cruise claims are heard in Miami, where we are based, so we can represent you wherever you live or sailed. The sooner you call, the more we can do.
