About Us

Cruise injuries are not ordinary injury cases.

The ticket in your inbox is a contract. It can limit where you sue, shorten how long you have to file, and shape what the cruise line will offer. Cruise Injury Advocates is the cruise-injury practice of Suro & Rodriguez, PLLC, a Miami-based trial firm representing injured passengers and crew nationwide and worldwide.

Clients we serve
Maritime law, the cruise line's ticket contract, and tight filing deadlines all change how your case must be handled. Most cruise lines also require claims to be filed in Miami, which is exactly where we are based, so we can represent injured passengers and crew no matter where you live or where you sailed.
From the first call we handle the cruise line and its insurers directly, move fast to preserve evidence, and prepare every claim as if it will be tried.
Cruise tickets often shorten the time you have to file and require notice well before suit. The sooner you call, the more we can do.
