About

The cruise-injury practice of Suro & Rodriguez, PLLC, a Miami-based trial firm representing passengers and crew nationwide and worldwide.

Who We Are

Cruise injuries are not ordinary injury cases.

The ticket in your inbox is a contract. It can limit where you sue, shorten how long you have to file, and shape what the cruise line will offer. Miss a deadline buried in that fine print and a strong case can disappear.

Cruise Injury Advocates is the cruise-injury practice of Suro & Rodriguez, PLLC, a Miami-based trial firm representing injured passengers and crew nationwide and worldwide. Because cruise lines usually require claims to be filed in Miami, you don't need to live here for us to represent you. From the first call, our attorneys handle the cruise line and its insurers directly, move quickly to preserve evidence, and build every claim as if it will be tried.

Decades of federal and jury-trial experience. Cruise lines tend to offer more when they know we will try the case.
Ticket contracts, forum-selection clauses, shortened deadlines, and the defense tactics the major lines use, this is the work we do.
Full representation in English and Spanish, with attorneys reachable for new injuries.
